Who I Am

I was born and raised in Virginia Beach, got a Bachelors in Systems Engineering from George Mason University in Washington D.C.

After working as a Systems Engineer for the MITRE corporation for a couple of years, I found my second half. We wanted a change so we decided to experience life in a foreign country. We lived in Saudi Arabia for six years.

In Saudi, I spent my time studying the Arabic language and religion. I graduated with a diploma in Arabic Language Studies from Imam Saud University in the capital city of Riyadh. It was a life changing experience.

After returning to the states, we settled in the Dallas / Ft. Worth area to live close to my wfie's family.

I've spent my time getting back in the swing of things, learning more about IT, Cloud, and most recently, Coding at Lambda School.

I love learning. I also like road biking, and skiing.
